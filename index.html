App Features

Weather Monitor helps you stay prepared by providing accurate, real-time weather updates:

  • 🌦 Live local weather updates
  • 📍 Location-based forecasts
  • 🌡 Switch between °C / °F
  • 📊 Hourly & 7-day forecast
  • 🔔 Severe weather alerts
  • ☀️ Weather widgets for quick glance
  • 🌙 Night mode for low light

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the weather not updating?

Ensure your internet connection is active and location services are turned on.

How do I change temperature units?

Go to Settings → Units and switch between °C or °F.

Can I get severe weather alerts?

Yes! Turn on notifications in the app’s settings to receive alerts.

Can I use the app offline?

Some features like live updates require internet, but cached forecasts can be seen offline.

How do I reset app settings?

Go to Settings → Reset App to restore defaults.

How accurate are the forecasts?

Weather Monitor uses trusted APIs for real-time updates, but forecasts may vary depending on location and data availability.

Can I customize notifications?

Yes! In Settings → Notifications, you can choose alerts for rain, storms, or temperature changes.
